What is IGME 230?

IGME 230, otherwise known as "Web Design and Implementation", is a course that aims to teach students basic to somewhat advanced concepts of web development. This page is dedicated to showcasing the work I completed while talking the course. To the left are links to each exercise and project that has been (or while be) completed.
